About myself

My name is Temurbek Sabirov. I am from country located in middle Asia called Uzbekistan. I have moved to United States when I was 16 years old.

My desire for learning new technology has been evolving ever since I was in elementary school. I used to experiement with random software I had on my Personal Computer. I did not know what the purpose of software I was using was but it did not stop me. I would tinker with the functionality until I got something that looked like work or until i broke or crashed the program. My brother would get angry at me everytime I crashed software like Photoshop or Sony Vegas. However, failing is a part of learning and as a result of failing in my early ages, I feel confident using software to do what I want, including video/photo editting or writing bits of code to create another software.

Moving to America opened a lot more doors for me to experiement and tinker with software. I am grateful for this opportunity to further enhance my skills and knowledge of using various types of software.

High School

After I moved to America, I went to highschool where I learned more from my classmates than the actual classwork. My main goal was to learn English. With zero knowledge in English, I was left to battle my way through many obstacles. I only had few classmates who spoke my language. The problem was, we barely had any classes together. Therefore, I was forced to communicate with others in English. Of course, it was not easy but with enough support and understanding from my classmates, I was able to learn the language and speak my mind without thinking too much or using too much hand gestures.

I did learn school material with the help of my teachers and homework they gave us but I did not find it so efficient. Not everyone learns the same way and I believe we should not be taught the same way. I know that it is easier said than done. However, I believe that hopefully in near future, our education system will be different in highschools so everyone can benefit from education.

Regardless of how my experience was in high school, I very much appreciate the people that were in this journey with me since the beginning.

College

I was accepted to many awesome colleges due to my highschool grades and SAT scores. One of those colleges were Brooklyn College.

The reason why I chose Brooklyn College over other colleges was because when I was in highschool, I took a college now class in Brooklyn College. I used to go to Brooklyn College twice a week and loved the enironments and the people it provides.

My journey for programming started as soon as a I stepped into my first class of Into to Java. At first, I was completely lost because I did not know what to expect. It was a completely new experience for me. As I struggled my way through my programming classes I made amazing friends with who I was able to battle through the semester. Yet again, with the help of my friends, I was able to learn a lot in the field of programming.

Although, Brooklyn College has taught me and has been teaching me for a lot ever since I step a foot in its gates, most of what I have learned was thanks to my friends.
