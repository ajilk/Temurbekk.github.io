What would you recommend someone who just started programming?

I am far from calling myself someone who has been in this field for a long time. I am a newcomer just like most of us. But one piece of advice that I would like to spread is to "Just do it." Many people make it sound like Programming is the hardest thing in the world. If they taught kids programming from early age like they teach English, we would all be programmers. It is about dedication and believing in yourself
